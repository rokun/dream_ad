Nigeria

Nigeria is a land flowing with opportunities. Since our mantra at Dreamweaver Advertising is that possibilities can be created, we search for unharnessed potentials in and outside the United Kingdom. Marketing opportunities abound in Nigeria for businesses around the world. At D.A, we discover those opportunities and turn them into profitable ventures for clients. We also do the same in the United Kingdom for Nigerian businesses. Two sectors in Nigeria of particular interest to D.A are the Financial and Educational Sectors. We have a strong network of Nigerian professionals and business executives that serve as consultants for Dreamweaver Advertising.

Corporate Social Responsibility

Our interest in Nigeria is not only the discovery of untapped potential. D.A is passionate about giving back and has strong interests in investing in educational development in Nigeria, especially in the far northern states of the country. Investments brought into the country are to enhance economic development as well as improve the social wellbeing of citizens. D.A is also interested in making contributions to foundations and charity organisations dedicated to caring for orphans and the homeless.
